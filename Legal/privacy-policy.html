Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to WizWeb! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Wallet addresses, usernames, and profile details.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the platform, such as posts, communities joined, and tokens earned.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the WizWeb platform.
  • To secure your account and ensure safe transactions.
  • To analyze platform usage and improve user experience.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service providers to maintain and enhance our platform.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data and request corrections.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and significant updates will be communicated via email or on the platform.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@wizweb.com
